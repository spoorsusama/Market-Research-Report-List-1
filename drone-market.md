<p><p>The drone market is categorized into three main types: fixed-wing, spiral wing, and helicopter drones. Fixed-wing drones are ideal for long-range surveillance and agricultural monitoring, featuring aerodynamic designs for efficient flight. Spiral wing drones, characterized by their unique rotor arrangements, offer enhanced stability and maneuverability, suitable for aerial photography and reconnaissance. Helicopter drones provide vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them versatile for urban environments and complex missions, such as search and rescue operations and infrastructure inspections.</p></p>
