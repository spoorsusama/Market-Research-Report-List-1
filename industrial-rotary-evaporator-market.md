<p><p>The industrial rotary evaporator market is divided into two primary types based on adjustment mechanisms: manual adjustment and electric adjustment. Manual adjustment rotary evaporators require users to physically manipulate knobs or levers to control parameters like rotation speed and temperature. In contrast, electric adjustment models automate these controls, offering precision and ease of use through digital interfaces. Electric adjustment systems enhance efficiency and consistency in processes, making them increasingly popular in laboratories and industries demanding high accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
