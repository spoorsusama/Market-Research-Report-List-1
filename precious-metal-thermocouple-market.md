S Type thermocouples, also made from platinum and rhodium, are acclaimed for their accuracy in high-temperature applications. B Type thermocouples, featuring a higher ratio of rhodium to platinum, excel in extreme temperature environments while providing excellent performance. Each type caters to specific industrial applications requiring precise thermal measurements.</p></p>
