<p><strong>The Automotive Side Airbags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Torso Airbags</li><li>Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side airbags market consists primarily of two types: side torso airbags and side tubular (curtain) airbags. Side torso airbags are designed to protect the upper body, primarily the torso, during side-impact collisions by inflating between the door and the occupant. In contrast, side tubular airbags, also known as curtain airbags, deploy from the roof area to shield the head and neck, offering enhanced protection against side impacts and rollover accidents, thereby improving overall passenger safety.</p></p>
