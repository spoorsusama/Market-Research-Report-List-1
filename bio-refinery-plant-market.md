<p><strong>The Bio-Refinery Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biochemical</li><li>Thermochemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-refinery plant market is categorized into two primary types: biochemical and thermochemical. Biochemical bio-refineries utilize biological processes, such as fermentation and enzymatic conversion, to transform biomass into valuable products like biofuels, biochemicals, and bioplastics. In contrast, thermochemical bio-refineries employ heat-based technologies, including gasification and pyrolysis, to convert biomass into energy-rich gases, bio-oils, and char. Both methods aim to maximize the extraction of energy and materials from renewable biomass, supporting sustainable practices in the bioeconomy.</p></p>
