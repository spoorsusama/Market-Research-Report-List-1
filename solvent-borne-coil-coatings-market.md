<p><strong>The Solvent-borne Coil Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primer Coatings</li><li>Back Coatings</li><li>Top Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent-borne coil coatings comprise three main types: primer coatings, back coatings, and top coatings. Primer coatings are applied to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance on metal surfaces. Back coatings serve as a protective layer on the reverse side of the metal, preventing damage and corrosion. Top coatings provide the final aesthetic finish and protect against environmental factors, such as UV radiation and weathering. Together, these coatings ensure durability, functionality, and visual appeal for various metal applications.</p></p>
