<p><p>The dry coolers market is primarily segmented into V-shaped and H-shaped dry coolers, each designed for efficient heat exchange in various industrial applications. V-shaped dry coolers feature a compact, space-saving design that allows for optimal airflow and cooling performance, making them ideal for areas with limited space. In contrast, H-shaped dry coolers offer a larger surface area and enhanced cooling efficiency, catering to applications requiring higher thermal loads. Both types serve essential roles in HVAC, refrigeration, and process cooling systems.</p></p>
