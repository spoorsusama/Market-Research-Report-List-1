<p><strong>The High Temperature Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane Anemometer</li><li>Cup Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature anemometer market is primarily segmented into two types: vane anemometers and cup anemometers. Vane anemometers utilize a rotating blade to measure wind speed and direction, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, cup anemometers feature three or four cups mounted on arms that spin with airflow, providing accurate measurements of wind speed, especially in outdoor settings. Both types are essential for monitoring high-temperature environments in industries like meteorology, HVAC, and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
