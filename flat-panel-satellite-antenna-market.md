<p><ul><li>Electronically Controlled Antenna (ESA)</li><li>Mechanically Controlled Antenna (MSA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flat Panel Satellite Antenna market is categorized into Electronically Controlled Antennas (ESA) and Mechanically Controlled Antennas (MSA). ESA utilizes advanced technology for automatic signal tracking and adjustment, enabling faster and more efficient communication with satellites, making them ideal for mobile and high-demand applications. In contrast, MSA relies on mechanical systems to achieve signal alignment, often requiring manual intervention and adjustment. While ESAs offer greater flexibility and performance, MSAs are typically more cost-effective for simpler applications.</p></p>
