<p><strong>The Gonorrhea Testing Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleic Acid Amplification (NAA)</li><li>Gram Stain</li><li>Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA)</li><li>Gonorrhea Culture</li><li>Rapid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gonorrhea testing kits market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Nucleic Acid Amplification (NAA)** tests, which detect genetic material from the bacteria, offering high sensitivity and specificity.</p><p>2. **Gram Stain** involves staining and microscopically identifying Neisseria gonorrhoeae, though it's less common due to variable accuracy.</p><p>3. **Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)** tests detect antibodies or antigens related to the infection.</p><p>4. **Gonorrhea Culture** involves growing the bacteria in a lab for confirmation, providing antibiotic susceptibility data.</p><p>5. **Rapid Tests** provide quick results, often in a point-of-care setting, allowing for immediate treatment decisions.</p></p>
