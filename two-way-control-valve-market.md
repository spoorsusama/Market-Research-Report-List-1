<p><strong>The Two-way Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-way Globe Valve</li><li>Two-way Butterfly Vavle</li><li>Two-way Ball Vavle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-way control valve market comprises three main types: two-way globe valves, two-way butterfly valves, and two-way ball valves. Two-way globe valves offer precise flow control due to their linear motion, making them ideal for throttling applications. Two-way butterfly valves are characterized by their quarter-turn operation and are suitable for larger flow volumes with reduced space requirements. Two-way ball valves provide quick shut-off capabilities and are known for their tight sealing, making them efficient for on-off control in various industries.</p></p>
