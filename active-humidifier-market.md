<p><p>The active humidifier market comprises two primary types: stand-alone humidifiers and integrated humidifiers. Stand-alone humidifiers function independently and are designed to add moisture to a specific area, ideal for personal or small room use. On the other hand, integrated humidifiers are built into HVAC systems, providing whole-house humidity control. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, with stand-alone models offering portability and convenience, while integrated systems deliver consistent humidity levels throughout larger spaces.</p></p>
