<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Emergency Contraceptives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combined estrogen and progestin pills</li><li>Progestin-only (as Levonorgestrel) pills</li><li>Antiprogestin (as mifepristone) pills</li><li>Ulipristal acetate pills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency contraceptives are categorized into several types based on their active ingredients. Combined estrogen and progestin pills inhibit ovulation and alter the uterine lining. Progestin-only pills, often containing Levonorgestrel, primarily prevent fertilization. Antiprogestin pills, like mifepristone, block progesterone to disrupt implantation. Ulipristal acetate pills function as a selective progesterone receptor modulator, delaying ovulation. Each type offers varying efficacy and mechanisms, catering to different needs and preferences in emergency contraception.</p></p>
