<p><strong>The Fire Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Fire Protection Systems</li><li>Passive Fire Protection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire protection systems market comprises two main types: active and passive fire protection systems. Active fire protection systems involve mechanisms that actively suppress or control fires, such as sprinklers, fire alarms, and extinguishers. In contrast, passive fire protection systems focus on containing fires and preventing their spread through structural measures, including fire-resistant materials, compartmentalization, and smoke barriers. Together, these systems enhance safety and minimize damage, catering to diverse needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
