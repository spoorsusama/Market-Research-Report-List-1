<p><strong>The Automotive Defroster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Defogger</li><li>Secondary Defogger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive defroster market comprises two primary types: the primary defogger and the secondary defogger. The primary defogger is integrated into the vehicle's HVAC system, directly using heated air to clear fog from the windshield and side windows. In contrast, the secondary defogger operates as an additional system, often utilizing features like rear-window heating or additional airflow mechanisms to enhance visibility. Both systems aim to improve driver safety and visibility by effectively managing humidity and condensation within the vehicle.</p></p>
