<p><ul><li>Single-cell Protection IC</li><li>Multi-cell Protection IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lithium Ion Battery Protection IC market is divided into two main types: Single-cell Protection ICs and Multi-cell Protection ICs. Single-cell Protection ICs are designed to monitor and safeguard individual battery cells, ensuring optimal performance and preventing overcharging or deep discharging. In contrast, Multi-cell Protection ICs are used in battery packs containing multiple cells, managing voltage and current levels across the cells to maintain overall pack safety and efficiency. Both types are critical for enhancing battery lifespan and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
