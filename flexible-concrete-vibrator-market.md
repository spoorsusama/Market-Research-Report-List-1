<p><p>The flexible concrete vibrator market is segmented into internal vibrators, external vibrators, and others. Internal vibrators are used to consolidate concrete from within the mixture, enhancing compaction and reducing air bubbles. External vibrators, applied on the surface, help in achieving proper settling and alignment without direct contact with the concrete. Other types may include specialized or innovative vibrators designed for specific applications. Each type caters to different needs in construction, ensuring optimal concrete quality and performance.</p></p>
