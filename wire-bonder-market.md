<p><p>The wire bonder market is categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic wire bonders offer high-speed, precision bonding with minimal operator intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. Semi-automatic models combine automation with manual oversight, allowing for flexibility in smaller runs while maintaining efficiency. Manual wire bonders require significant operator skill, typically used for prototyping or low-volume tasks, providing hands-on control and adaptability but at a slower pace compared to automated alternatives.</p></p>
