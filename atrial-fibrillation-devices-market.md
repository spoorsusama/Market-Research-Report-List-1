<p><p>The atrial fibrillation devices market encompasses various modalities for managing this common heart condition. Catheter ablation involves the use of catheters to destroy abnormal heart tissue that causes irregular rhythms, providing a minimally invasive solution. Maze surgery, on the other hand, is a surgical procedure that creates a "maze" of incisions in the heart to redirect electrical impulses. Both approaches aim to restore normal heart rhythm, offering effective treatment options for patients suffering from atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
