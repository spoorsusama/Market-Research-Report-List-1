<p><p>The Sleep Non-invasive Ventilator (NIV) market primarily includes CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) ventilators. CPAP devices deliver a steady airflow to keep airways open during sleep, typically used for obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, BiPAP devices provide two pressure settings: a higher pressure during inhalation and a lower pressure during exhalation, catering to patients with more complex respiratory needs. Both options play crucial roles in improving sleep quality and overall health in affected individuals.</p></p>
