<p><p>The sublimation printing equipment market consists of three main types: printers, heat presses, and consumables. Printers specifically designed for sublimation use dye-based inks to transfer vibrant designs onto substrates, such as fabric or ceramics. Heat presses apply the necessary heat and pressure to permanently bond the sublimation dye to the material. Consumables include inks, transfer papers, and other materials that facilitate the sublimation process. Together, these components enable high-quality printing on various items, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
