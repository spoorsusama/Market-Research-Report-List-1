<p><p>Cloud billing encompasses various market types catering to different payment mechanisms. Subscription billing allows customers to pay a fixed fee at regular intervals for continuous service access. Metered billing charges based on actual usage, adjusting costs dynamically. Cloud service billing focuses on aggregating fees for multiple cloud services. Provisioning refers to billing related to resource allocation and setup. Additionally, other market types may include one-time charges or hybrid models, catering to diverse consumer needs in the cloud ecosystem.</p></p>
