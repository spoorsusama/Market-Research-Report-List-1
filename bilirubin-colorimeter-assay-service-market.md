<p><strong>The Bilirubin Colorimeter Assay Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conjugated Bilirubin (Direct Bilirubin)</li><li>Unconjugated Bilirubin (Indirect Bilirubin)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bilirubin Colorimeter Assay Service market is segmented into two primary types: Conjugated Bilirubin (Direct Bilirubin) and Unconjugated Bilirubin (Indirect Bilirubin). Conjugated Bilirubin refers to the water-soluble form that is excreted in bile, typically elevated in liver dysfunction. Unconjugated Bilirubin, the fat-soluble form, accumulates due to hemolysis or ineffective erythropoiesis. These assays are vital for diagnosing and monitoring liver diseases, hemolytic anemias, and other related conditions, ensuring accurate evaluation of bilirubin levels in patients.</p></p>
