<p><p>The coronary guidewires market primarily consists of two main types: steel-based and nitinol-based guidewires. Steel-based guidewires are known for their strength and rigidity, making them suitable for navigating complex vascular structures. In contrast, nitinol-based guidewires offer superior flexibility and torque response due to their unique shape-memory alloy properties, allowing for enhanced maneuverability in tortuous vessels.
