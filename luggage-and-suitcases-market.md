<p><p>The luggage and suitcases market is primarily divided into two types: hardsided and softsided. Hardsided luggage features a rigid exterior, providing better protection for fragile items and enhanced security against theft. It is often made from materials like polycarbonate or aluminum, ensuring durability. In contrast, softsided luggage is flexible, made from fabric materials like nylon or polyester, allowing for more packing space. Softsided options are generally lighter and more adaptable but may offer less protection. Each type caters to different traveler needs and preferences.</p></p>
