<p><p>The oil filtration system market encompasses various types, notably centrifugal and vacuum filtration systems, along with others. Centrifugal systems use centrifugal force to separate contaminants from oil, making them efficient for high-viscosity fluids. Vacuum filtration systems employ reduced pressure to draw oil through a filter, effectively removing impurities. Other types may include depth filters and coalescing filters, catering to specific industrial needs. Each system is designed to enhance oil purity, extend equipment life, and improve operational efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
