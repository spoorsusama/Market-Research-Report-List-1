<p><p>Capacitive level sensors are categorized into two main types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors detect when a specific level is reached, triggering an output, ideal for applications requiring on/off control. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, provide real-time level measurement, delivering ongoing data on liquid levels for processes needing precise monitoring. Both types leverage capacitance change to detect levels, making them suitable for various industries, including water treatment, food and beverage, and manufacturing.</p></p>
