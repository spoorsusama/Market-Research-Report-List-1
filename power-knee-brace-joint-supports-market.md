<p><p>Power knee brace joint supports are categorized into two main types based on rebound characteristics: high rebound and low rebound. High rebound knee braces feature materials that quickly return to their original shape, providing dynamic support and facilitating greater mobility during physical activities. In contrast, low rebound knee braces offer more static support, absorbing shocks and stabilizing the knee joint without excessive movement. These differences cater to varying user needs, from active individuals seeking agility to those requiring enhanced stability and protection.</p></p>
