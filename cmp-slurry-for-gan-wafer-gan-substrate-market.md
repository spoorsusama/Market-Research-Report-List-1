<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurry for GaN wafers is essential for achieving a smooth surface and enhancing device performance. Colloidal silica polishing slurry is known for its fine abrasive particles, providing superior planarization and surface finish, making it ideal for the final polishing stages. In contrast, alumina polishing slurry employs coarser particles and is effective for initial polishing and thick material removal. Both types play crucial roles in GaN substrate processing, impacting the efficiency and yield of semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
