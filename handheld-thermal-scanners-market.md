<p><strong>The Handheld Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld thermal scanners market is segmented into three main types based on their infrared wavelength: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners are effective for applications like humidity measurement and material identification due to their sensitivity to water content. MWIR scanners are used for thermal imaging and surveillance, benefiting from superior image quality. LWIR scanners excel in applications such as building inspections and energy audits, detecting heat patterns to identify thermal losses.</p></p>
