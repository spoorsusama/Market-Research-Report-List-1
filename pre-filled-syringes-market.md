<p><p>The pre-filled syringes market comprises two main types: glass and plastic pre-filled syringes. Glass pre-filled syringes are preferred for their inert nature, which minimizes interaction with the drug, ensuring stability and compatibility. They are often used for high-value biologics and sensitive medications. In contrast, plastic pre-filled syringes offer advantages such as lighter weight, reduced breakage risk, and ease of handling. They cater to a broader range of applications, including vaccines and injectable pharmaceuticals, enhancing patient convenience and safety.</p></p>
