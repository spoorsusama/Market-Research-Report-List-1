<p><strong>The LVDT Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) displacement sensors operate in two primary market types: contact and non-contact. Contact LVDTs measure displacement by direct physical interaction with the object, often providing precise measurements in controlled environments but facing wear over time. Non-contact LVDTs utilize electromagnetic principles to measure displacement without physical contact, offering durability and eliminating issues related to friction or wear. Both types are essential in various applications, including industrial automation, aerospace, and medical equipment, providing reliable and accurate displacement measurements.</p></p>
