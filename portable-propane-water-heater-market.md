<p><p>The portable propane water heater market is divided into two main types: tank and tankless models. Tank models store a specific volume of heated water, making them suitable for consistent hot water supply, but they may take longer to refill once depleted. In contrast, tankless models heat water on demand, providing an endless supply without the need for a storage tank, making them more energy-efficient and space-saving. Both options cater to different needs and preferences in portable hot water solutions.</p></p>
