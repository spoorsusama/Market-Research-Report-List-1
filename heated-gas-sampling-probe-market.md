<p><p>The heated gas sampling probe market is categorized into two main types: straight gas sampling probes and curved gas sampling probes. Straight gas sampling probes are designed for direct access to gas streams and are typically easier to install in straight pipes or ducts. In contrast, curved gas sampling probes are engineered for applications requiring flexible access in confined spaces or where pipes are not straight. Both types are essential for ensuring accurate gas sampling while maintaining temperature control to prevent condensation and contamination.</p></p>
