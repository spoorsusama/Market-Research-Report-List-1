<p><p>Tablet counters are classified into three main market types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic tablet counters enable high-speed counting and packaging with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and precision. Semi-automatic counters require some manual input but still streamline the counting process, balancing automation and user control. Manual counters rely entirely on human operation, suitable for smaller production runs or varying tablet types. Each type serves distinct operational needs across pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and other industries that require precise counting and packaging.</p></p>
