<p><strong>The Thin Film Thermal Printhead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corner Type</li><li>Side Face Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thin film thermal printhead market is categorized primarily into two types: corner type and side face type. Corner type printheads feature a layout where the heating elements are positioned at the corners, optimizing space and enhancing print quality. In contrast, side face type printheads have heating elements aligned along the sides, providing a wider printing area and improved thermal conductivity. Each type serves different applications, with specific advantages in terms of size, efficiency, and print resolution, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
