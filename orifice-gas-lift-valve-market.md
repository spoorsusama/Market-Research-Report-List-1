<p><strong>The Orifice Gas Lift Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Flow</li><li>Intermittent Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Orifice Gas Lift Valve market is divided into two key types: Continuous Flow and Intermittent Flow. Continuous Flow valves enable a steady stream of gas, maintaining a consistent lift in oil production, making them suitable for stable reservoir conditions. In contrast, Intermittent Flow valves operate in cycles, releasing gas only when needed, which can optimize production in fluctuating reservoir pressures. Each type caters to different operational needs, enhancing efficiency and productivity in oil extraction processes.</p></p>
