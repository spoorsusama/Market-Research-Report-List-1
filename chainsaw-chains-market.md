<p><p>Chainsaw chains are categorized primarily into two types: full chisel and semi-chisel. Full chisel chains feature square-cornered teeth, offering fast cutting in softwoods, making them ideal for professional loggers. In contrast, semi-chisel chains have rounded teeth, which maintain sharpness longer and perform better in dirty or abrasive conditions, catering to both professionals and homeowners. The market dynamics for these chains reflect varying consumer needs based on cutting efficiency, durability, and application, influencing preferences across different segments.</p></p>
