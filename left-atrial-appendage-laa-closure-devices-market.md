<p><strong>The Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epicardial LAA Closure Devices</li><li>Endocardial LAA Closure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure Devices market is divided into two main types: epicardial and endocardial devices. Epicardial LAA closure devices are implanted on the outer surface of the heart, offering direct access and control during the procedure. In contrast, endocardial LAA closure devices are introduced through the heart's interior, typically via catheterization, allowing for minimally invasive procedures. Both types aim to reduce the risk of thrombus formation and subsequent stroke in patients with atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
